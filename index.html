Personal Medical Cost Visualization
by Insurance Forecast

This project visualizes a Kaggle dataset on health insurance costs, exploring factors like demographics and habits. The interactive visualizations help users understand trends and patterns, potentially guiding their insurance policy decisions. By analyzing factors such as age, sex, BMI, children, region, and smoking habits, stakeholders can identify areas for intervention and policy changes. This analysis aims to aid decision-making and promote more effective insurance policies.
